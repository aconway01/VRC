Pari-Mutuel Wagering Business Relationship Certification

This certification verifies that a Vendor Applicant has an established or proposed business relationship with a Licensed Pari-Mutuel Wagering Facility or Applicant authorized by the Virginia Racing Commission (VRC).

Vendor Permit Application

A vendor must hold an approved permit from the VRC before engaging in business with any licensed pari-mutuel wagering facility in Virginia.
